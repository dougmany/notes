Thala Guli Recipe | Srilankan Sesame Balls/Rolls

Narmadha

Thali Guli is Srilankan sweet recipe made with coconut, sesame seeds and jaggery.

SERVINGS

20 -25 small balls

INGREDIENTS

  • 1x
  • 2x
  • 3x
  • Sesame seeds ½ cup
  • Desiccated coconut ½ cup
  • Powdered Jaggery ⅓ cup
  • Salt a pinch

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Dry roast sesame seeds till it pops up.
  2. Dry roast the coconut till slightly brown.
  3. Take sesame seeds, coconut and grated jaggery in a mixer.
  4. Pulse them 2-3 times till combined.
  5. Take the mixture in bowl.
  6. Shape them as small balls or cylindrical rolls.
  7. Repeat the same for rest of the mixture.
  8. Thala Guli/Sesame rolls is ready.

NOTES

Traditionally they use kithul jaggery in Srilanka for making this sweet. You can use any type of jaggery.

Shape them as you wish

I used fresh coconut and roasted them nicely. Desiccated coconut is preferred for more shelf life.

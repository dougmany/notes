Feijoa Cake

Cake

  • 5.3 tbs butter, sofened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup mashed feijoas
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tbs vanilla essence
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  • Cream butter with sugar, blend in feijoas.
  • Heat milk, add baking soda. Add to above mixture, along with egg and vanilla.
  • Sift flour and baking powder, mix into wet ingredients.
  • Pour into greased, 20 cm cake tin and bake at 356Â°F for 40 minutes.

Icing

  • 200g cream cheese
  • 200g icing sugar
  • 1 tbs butter
  • lemon juice

Steps

  • Beat cream cheese until soft, add icing sugar.
  • Add lemon and butter, blend until smooth.

Tarka

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 green onions chopped (separate green from white)
  • 2 tbs Ginger (3 in piece), minced
  • 6 cloved garlic, minced
  • 2-3 serrano chili minced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 tbs canned diced tomatos
  • 2 tsp ground tumeric
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Cook onions and white part of green onions in butter until starts to brown.
  • Add ginger, garlic, chili, and cumin seeds.
  • Cook until you cannot see garlic. Add green onion tops, both tomatos and masala.
  • Cook until golden, add splash of water if dry.

Targa adjustments:
Chicken: 50% more chili and canned tomato, oil replaces butter.
Ground turkey: no canned tomatos, 2/3 * garlic and ginger, oil replaces butter.
Vegetables: cook 1/2 as long, no paprika, no tomatos, thinly slice onion.

Rice

  • 2 tbs oil
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 3 cups rice
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 5.5 cups water (r:w = 1.5 : 2.75)
  • cilantro
  • Heat oil and cook onions until soft. Add Peas, carrots and cumin seeds.
  • Add rice and stir to coat. Add water and bring to boil.
  • Stir and reduce to med-high 5-10 minutes with lit tilted.
  • When water is gone, fully cover and put on lowest heat for 5 minutes

Lentils

  • 1 cup lentils (Moth)
  • 6 cups water (for flat lentils on stove)*
  • Tarka
  • Add Lentils to water and cook 40 minutes
  • Add Targa, simmer until thick.

*Round lentil water: 7 cups (stove) or 6 cups (pressure cooker.)

Chicken

  • 7 breasts or whole chicken
  • Tarka
  • 6 cups water
  • 1/2 C cream, yogurt or coconut milk
  • cilantro
  • Sear chicken, add targa.
  • Add water and boil for 30-40 minutes.
  • Add cream, cook 5 minutes, serve with cilantro.

Ground Turkey

  • Tarka
  • 1.5 lbs ground turkey
  • 1.5 cups peas
  • 1 yukon gold potato diced
  • cilantro
  • Cook turkey until brown. Add targa and stir.
  • Add peas and cook for 5 minutes. Add potoato, cook until soft server with cilantro.

Cauliflower (or any vegetables)

  • Tarka
  • 1 head cauliflower florets halved with stems attached
  • 3 yukon gold potatos cut into 6 pieces each
  • cilantro
  • Cook potatos with tarka, 5-7 minutes, covered.
  • Add cauliflower and cook until soft. serve with cilantro

Thala Guli Recipe | Srilankan Sesame Balls/Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Sesame seeds
  • 1/2 cup Desiccated coconut
  • 1/3 cup Powdered Jaggery
  • pinch Salt

Steps

  • Dry roast sesame seeds untill they pop.
  • Dry roast the coconut untill slightly brown.
  • Take sesame seeds, coconut and grated jaggery in a mixer.
  • Pulse them 2-3 times till combined.
  • Take the mixture in bowl.
  • Shape them as small balls or cylindrical rolls.

Notes: Traditionally they use kithul jaggery in Srilanka for making this sweet. You can use any type of jaggery. Shape them as you wish. I used fresh coconut and roasted them nicely. Desiccated coconut is preferred for more shelf life
